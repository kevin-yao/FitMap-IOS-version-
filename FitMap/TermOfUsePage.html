Privacy Policy


Our privacy policy explains:
  • What information we collect and why we collect it
  • How we use that information.
  • Information we share

What information we collect

Location Information
When you use a location-enabled GPS, we may collect and process information about your actual location, like GPS signals sent by a mobile device. We may also use various technologies to determine location, such as sensor data from your device that may, for example, provide information on nearby Wi-Fi access points and cell towers.

Local Storage
We may collect and store information (including personal information) locally on your device using mechanisms such as browser web storage (including HTML 5), application data caches and core data.

Fitbit Information
When you choose to connect Fitbit Account, we will use your weight information and activity information including steps, calories and miles to integrate your data into our services

How we use that information

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them. The mainly use of your information is the location and your personal activity data.

Information we share

Fitbit
Since we apply Fitbit API, we may share your location information to Fitbit, however, since they use their own protection, your information is safe too, for detailed information. Check Fitbit Privacy Policy

Apple Maps
We use Apple Maps framework to show you map information, hence, your information will be shared to Maps, but your information is safe under their protection too. check Maps Privacy Policy
